Creative Fashion Afrika. Afrika To The World.

The Creative Fashion Afrika website will enable the fashion organization to reach out to the African community and provide a platform for which African youths can explore and share their fashion ideas through clothing, footwear, lifestyle, accessories, makeup, hairstyle etc. The website will act as a public forum for examination of important, contemporary issues in the business of fashion in Africa and present timely information regarding trends that have an effect on the fashion industry.

Discover a modern African inspired fashion, all in colors and for all seasons: dashiki dress, African dress, wax print skirts, African crop top, sarouel.. These collections fit all women: plus size women, pregnant women... But there is also baby and men African fashion: polos, African inspired shirts, bootee, baby changing material ... And for everyday such as pencil skirt in african fabric for work, kente cocktail dress for your evening , just as batik headwraps and clutch bags, khanga or dashiki accessories



Harnessing the Creativity of the African Fashion Industry to provide youth with skills and employment by focusing on the potential of Africa's fashion industry. Africa thrives on rich colors, cultural diversity, and traditional handicrafts that live on until today thanks to wonderful creatives. Creative Fashion Afrika creates a cross-cultural dialogue through fashion by reinterpreting African traditions with contemporary designs and cuts.

Creative Fashion Afrika federates a large choice of african inspired decoration items , with differents african fabrics or precious materials. These goes from the traditional masks, statues or sculptures to the more modern african acrylic paintings, colorful handmade drawings , leather , ebony or wood boxes, or oil paintings. The artist use traditional african fabrics such as bogolan, handtainted bazin , handwoven african fabrics or batiks.

The platform hopes to offer both an editorial view of global African style and an e-commerce arm selling a mix of African-produced and inspired brands, including artisanal gems scouted from across the continent by the best in the fashion industry.
